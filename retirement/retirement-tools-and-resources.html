Retirement tools and resources.

Got a burning retirement question or just looking for some more information? We've compiled common questions and handy tools to help you find the answers you need.

Whether you are 20, 30 or 50, planning for your retirement can seem like a huge undertaking.  But don’t worry, we’re here to help. If you can't find the answers you're looking for here, give us a call on 1300 348 546.

Retirement advice

The key to making the most of your retirement is to take the time to plan and build a solid financial foundation. It may sound like a lot to take on but it doesn’t take much, particularly with Hostplus guiding you through the process. 

Questions? We have answers

When it comes to planning for your retirement there’s no such thing as a silly question. In fact it’s more than likely someone else has asked the same thing before you.

If you don’t find the answers you need here or would like to discuss things further, feel free to call  us so we can help you out. 

Forms and brochures

If you’ve misplaced a form or just want to find out more about our services, you can download and print any of our forms and brochures here.  

Don't forget, there's a range of transactions you can perform online too, just log in to your account whenever you need to.

 Of course, if you’d like a form or brochure mailed to you or need some help then don’t hesitate to give us a call on 1300 348 546.

Take the step. Open a Hostplus Pension account

Starting a Hostplus Pension account puts you in control of your financial future. Join today to discover just how rewarding your retirement can be when you have Hostplus at your side.
